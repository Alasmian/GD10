Space Shuttle Game

Instructions

In this game, you will control a space shuttle navigating through the vast multiverse. Your mission is to defeat the shuttle enemy while dodging their attacks.

Game Controls:

  • Spacebar: Fire your laser to attack the enemy shuttle.
  • Left Arrow: Turn your shuttle left.
  • Right Arrow: Turn your shuttle right.

Game Objectives:

Your goal is to destroy the enemy shuttle by firing at it, dodging its attacks, and avoiding obstacles. The enemy will attack you with powerful shots, so be careful!
